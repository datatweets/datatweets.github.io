Hello, and welcome to my website! I am Mohammad Mehdi Lotfinejad, a Principal Lead Data Scientist and a Senior Data Engineer. I have dedicated the past decade of my career to harnessing the power of enterprise data, translating complex information into insightful, actionable strategies.

But my journey with data started much earlier. I am a seasoned academic with over 15 years of experience in computer science instruction and technical training in the industry. Teaching has taught me the importance of clear communication, and I’m proud to say that effective, accessible presentation of complex information is one of my strongest skills.

Beyond academia, I’ve applied my skills in the ever-evolving data science landscape. As a Lead Data Scientist, I have honed my capabilities in various tools and technologies, including Apache Hadoop, Apache Spark, RDBMS, NoSQL, and data visualization tools. I am also well-versed in cloud-based Data Warehouses such as Snowflake and Amazon Redshift and have implemented data pipelines using Apache Airflow and Amazon Data Pipeline.

My approach to data is pragmatic and solutions-oriented. I see data not as a static asset but as a dynamic resource capable of driving change and innovation. My focus is on finding answers and the right questions to ask.

To further augment my expertise, I pursued a certification in Business Analytics from Harvard Business School. This experience has deepened my understanding of how data analysis intersects with business strategy, allowing me to deliver impactful solutions that align with organizational objectives.

On this website, you will find a portfolio of my projects, a collection of my writings, and insights from my work. I hope they inspire you as much as my journey in data science has inspired me.

Welcome to my world of data!
